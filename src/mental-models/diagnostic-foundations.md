## How to Tell What's Wrong

When something breaks, the first question is: **where is the problem?**

This guide helps you systematically isolate whether your issue is:
- USD (scene structure)
- Physics (numerical/contact)
- Python (execution order/timing)
- Integration (ROS/external systems)
